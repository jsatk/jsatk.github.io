John Gruber's Old Fashion Recipe

February 24, 2015 1-minute read

As dictated by John Gruber in The Talk Show ep. 111

Ingredients

  • Two(2) small dashes of Fee Brothers’ Old Fashion Aromatic Bitters
  • Two(2) small dashes of Fee Brothers’ West Indian Orange Bitters
  • 1 sugar cube
  • 1 splash of Soda Stream
  • “Fist-full of cracked ice”
  • 2oz bourbon (Marco prefers Buffalo Trace)
  • Stir
  • Pour into glass with one(1) big ice cube
  • Lemon peel or Orange peel
